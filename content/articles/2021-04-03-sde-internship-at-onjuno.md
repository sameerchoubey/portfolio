I joined [OnJuno](https://onjuno.com) as an SDE Intern in November’20 (shortly before the public launch of the core product) and it’s been an amazing journey till now.

I am a firm believer in great products, so, when I saw the website of OnJuno for the first time; I really liked it. The next thing that I did was sent a DM to Varun Deshpande (our CEO) for an internship. He was kind enough to respond and schedule an interview for me.

Fast forward two days later, I got my interview scheduled with Ratnesh Ray (our CTO) and a couple more interviews later, I received an internship offer, which I accepted straight away.

Today marks the end of wonderful 5 months at OnJuno and in these 5 months, I have worked on a couple of things.

>a) I wrote a lot of Metabase queries (a lot, trust me).

>b) I worked on our internal CRM portal. This is still a work-in-progress, but, I along with my mentor Shubham Sharma built this for our customer support team. I got introduced to VueJS along the way and enjoyed it.

A huge thank you to my mentor [Shubham bhaiya](https://twitter.com/yes_shubham) for guiding me all along.

And last but not least, I have received an offer from OnJuno to join their team full time as a Software Development Engineer.

I also received three more tech offers from campus placements, but, I’ll be joining OnJuno after graduation.

Really really excited about what's ahead.
