![OnJuno](./onjuno.png)
I joined [OnJuno](https://onjuno.com/) as an SDE Intern in November'20 (shortly before the public launch of the core product) and it's been an amazing journey till now.

I am a firm believer in great products, so, when I saw OnJuno's website for the first time in October, the next thing that I knew was I contacted [Varun Deshpande](https://twitter.com/thebitsian) (our CEO) for an internship via Twitter DM. He was kind enough to respond and schedule an interview for me.

Fast forward two days later, I got my interview scheduled with [Ratnesh Ray](https://twitter.com/ray_ratnesh) (our CTO) and a couple more interviews later, I received an internship offer, which I accepted straight away after discussing it with my T&P cell.

Today, I have completed 5 months at OnJuno and in these 5 months, I have worked on a couple of things. 

> a) I wrote a lot of Metabase queries (a lot, trust me).

> b) I worked on our internal CRM portal. This is still a work-in-progress, but, I along with my mentor [Shubham Sharma](https://twitter.com/yes_shubham) built this for our customer support team. I got introduced to VueJS along the way and enjoyed it.

A huge thank you to my mentor Shubham bhaiya for guiding me all along. 

And last but not least, I have received a full-time offer from OnJuno.

I also received three more offers from campus placements, but, I'll be joining OnJuno as a Software Development Engineer after graduation.
